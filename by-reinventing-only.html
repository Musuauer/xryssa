By Reinventing Only

Public Intervention, 2015
Musrara Mix15, Interdiscplinary Art Festival, Jerusalem, Israel.

Two local musicians took part in the performance and occurred during the festival at different locations in the city. The performers had a yellow-colored hands and softly sang sentences from the third act of Shakespeare's Hamlet for pedestrians.

Performers: Lee Teperberg-Kannay, Schmuel Zeltser
Duration: 3 Days
Photos: Nadav Ariel
