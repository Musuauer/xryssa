From My West To Reach All The Way To The East

Performance, 2015
Duration: 4 hours
Audio on speakers: 3:37 min.
Scotty Enterprises, Project Space Festival, Berlin

For this work, which was inspired by Zelda Rhiando’s novel Caposcripti, I created a sound installation that was audible in the gallery and outside on the street. During the opening and at the last day of the exhibition, I presented a performance for which I invited people from different backgrounds to run, to pass through and by Scotty Enterprises in their attempt to escape, compete, arrive or recreate from their own realities.
