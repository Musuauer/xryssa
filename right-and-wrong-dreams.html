Right And Wrong Dreams

Performance, printed hats, 2015
Duration: 50 min.
Global Alien Anniversary, Galerie im Turm, Berlin

I asked the members of a Greek family living in Berlin to participate. The four family members were covered with a white cloth. They wandered together in the gallery space and shared something that they dreamed recently. The group stood a while next to the printed hats and then walked out to the public space.
