You know, You are right

Performance, 2016
HD Video, Sound
Athens
Duration: 2 min 38 sec

I was invited to create a performance for a video. I asked two friends of mine to participate in and to sit on a seesaw, one on each end, with one instruction: they had to work out how to make the seesaw balance.

Participants: Eva Kechagia, Apostolos Kitsos
Cinematography: Aegle Drakou

Video loop 2:38 min.
